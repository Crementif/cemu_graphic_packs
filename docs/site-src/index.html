    Official website of the Community Graphic Packs for Cemu, used to upscale and change your Wii U games.

    Downloads

    Automatic installation instructions (recommended)
    Use Cemu's build-in graphic packs downloader (a new feature added in Cemu 1.15.1) to automatically retrieve the newest graphic packs from our repository.
    Manual installation instructions
    Remove all of your graphic packs first, then move all of the folders from a downloaded .zip below to the graphicPacks folder from your Cemu folder.

    Select the download for your Cemu version

    Cemu 1.14.0 and newer (version 3)
    Cemu 1.8.0 through 1.13.2 (version 2) Cemu 1.7.0 through 1.7.5 (version 1)
